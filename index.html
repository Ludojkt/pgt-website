About PGT

A World-Class Joint Venture for Indonesia's Maritime Future

Three global and regional leaders combining port expertise, industrial capabilities, and deep knowledge of Indonesia's logistics landscape.

Patimban Global Gateway Terminal (PGT) is a deep-sea container terminal operator established to increase Indonesia's port capacities and address a constant growing market demand.

PGT is a long-term, 35-year sub-concessionaire entrusted by PT Pelabuhan Patimban Internasional (PPI).

Strategic Location

At the Crossroads of West Java's Industrial Belt

Positioned between Karawang and Subang industrial estates — Indonesia's fastest-growing manufacturing corridor.

Location Advantages

Why Patimban

A strategic alternative to congested Jakarta ports, offering faster access to West Java's industrial heartland.

Shorter Transit Times

Only 1.5–2 hours from Karawang by truck (vs. 4–6 hours to Tanjung Priok through Jakarta congestion).

Toll Road Connectivity

Direct access to Trans-Java corridor via the upcoming Patimban Toll Road (2027) — just 30 minutes from the main highway.

Zero Congestion

No congestion on approach roads or inside the port — unlike Jakarta's heavily saturated corridors.

Industrial Proximity

Close to automotive, electronics, FMCG, and manufacturing clusters across Karawang and Subang.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

🇪🇺 Under GDPR
  • Access — obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent at any time
🇮🇩 Under UU PDP No. 27/2022
  • Right to information about processing
  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction / update of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of data no longer necessary
